Shredded Potato Stir Fry

Description

Shredded potato stir fry is one of the most popular Chinese home dishes. This dish has a different texture to the normal french fries and has a spicy and tangy taste to it.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound russet potato
  • 1 large green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 4 dried red chiles
  • 4 teaspoons of rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of kosher salt

Steps

  1. Fill a medium sized bowl with cold water and start peeling the potatoes.
  2. Shred them with a mandoline or just cut them. You want them to be long thin matchsticks.
  3. Submerge them in water for about 30 minutes to rinse off excess starch.
  4. Cut the bell pepper in half lengthwise and discard the stems and seeds and cut them into the same long thin matchsticks.
  5. Pat the potatoes dry and add 2 teaspoons of oil in the pan.
  6. Add 2 of the chiles and stir them until they start to blacken.
  7. Add the potatoes and bell peppers and stir fry them vigorously.
  8. Sprinkle with salt as you stir fry. Once the stir fry is done, turn off the heat and add vinegar.
  9. Stir to mix well before serving the dish.
