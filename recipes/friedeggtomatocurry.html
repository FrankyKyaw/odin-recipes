Fried Egg Tomato Curry

Description

Fried egg tomato curry is a mixture of a normal fried egg with the Burmese style tomato curry.

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 2 medium sized onions
  • 1 tablespoon of tumeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon of chili powder
  • 1 medium sized tomato
  • 1 cup of water

Steps

  1. Fry the eggs and put them aside.
  2. Using the remaining oil, fry the chopped onions.
  3. Add the tumeric powder and fry until brown.
  4. Add the chili powder when the onions are brown.
  5. Put the chopped tomatoes in the pan.
  6. Pour some water and crush the tomatoes into smaller pieces
  7. Add a dash of salt and aginomoto.
  8. When the tomatoes and onions are properly cooked, add the fried eggs.
  9. Adjust the salt according to the taste.
  10. Enjoy this scrumptious meal.
